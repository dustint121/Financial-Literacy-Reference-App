Finance Formulas

  • Network 
     Network = Assets - Liabilities
  • Cash surplus/deficit
    •  Cash surplus/deficit = Cash inflow - Cash outflow
  • Debt ratio
    •  Debt ratio = Assets / Liabilities
  • Current ratio
    •  Current ratio = Liquid Assets / Current Liabilities
  • Liquidity ratio
    •  Liquidity ratio = Liquid Assets / Monthly Expenses
  • Debt-payments ratio
    •  Debt-payments ratio = Monthly credit payments / Take-home pay
  • Savings ratio
    •  Savings ratio = Amount saved per month / Gross Monthly Income
